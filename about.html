About Me

I also enjoy reading self-help books and exploring spiritual ideas, which help me stay grounded and reflective.

I thrive in team environments, and I believe that great ideas come to life when people collaborate with purpose and passion. Whether it’s a tech project, a programming contest, or a group run, I’m always excited to contribute, learn, and grow with others.
